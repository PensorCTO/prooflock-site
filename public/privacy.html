Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Our Pledge

We do not collect, track, or sell your personal information. You are not the product. ProofLock operates on a Zero-Knowledge architecture, meaning we never have access to your unencrypted files, wallet keys, or private data.

2. How We Use Your Email

Your email is used for one purpose only: to cryptographically generate your unique, encrypted Wallet ID. This allows you to anchor proofs to the Polygon blockchain without managing complex private keys manually. We never use your email for marketing, and we never sell it to third parties. Your email address is stored securely and used solely for authentication purposes.

3. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

ProofLock operates on a strict Zero-Knowledge principle. We cannot see, access, or recover your files, keys, or private data. All encryption and cryptographic operations happen locally on your device before any data transmission. The VaultService performs on-device encryption of your files before they are stored in your private vault. Your unencrypted files never leave your device in an unencrypted state.

4. Local Encryption & Secure Storage

VaultService performs encryption on-device before transmission. All files are encrypted locally using AES-256 encryption before being stored in your private vault. WalletService manages your cryptographic keys using the device's secure storage system (iOS Keychain/Secure Enclave, Android Keystore). Your private keys are never transmitted or stored on our servers. They remain exclusively on your device and are protected by hardware security modules when available.

5. Public Ledger Anonymity

The Polygon blockchain receives only a mathematical hash (SHA-256) of your file. Your filenames, metadata, file contents, and identity never leave your device in an unencrypted form. The blockchain ledger contains only cryptographic fingerprints that prove the existence and integrity of your files at a specific point in time, without revealing any file contents or personally identifiable information.

6. AI & Data Privacy

ProofLock does not share your personal data, files, or identity with third-party AI services. All cryptographic operations, file hashing, and encryption are performed locally on-device. We do not use machine learning or AI processing on your file contents, and we do not send your data to external AI services for analysis or processing.

7. No-Recovery Policy

Because we operate on Zero-Knowledge principles and do not store your encryption keys or wallet credentials, we cannot recover your account, reset your password, or restore your files if you lose access. Your wallet keys are stored exclusively on your device. We strongly recommend backing up your encrypted vault files and keeping your authentication credentials secure. ProofLock cannot assist with account recovery due to our Zero-Knowledge architecture.
